Here are some frequently asked questions about HZ:

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    You can use the HZ printers on campus. Log in with your HZ account in MyNetPay
    on the printer system, upload or send the document from your laptop,
    and then you can print it at any available HZ printer.

  2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    You can easily scan documents at HZ using one of the printers in the building.
    The scan will be sent to your HZ email address as a PDF. You need to be logged in
    with your HZ account to use this function.

  3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

    You can purchase items and event tickets through the HZ Webshop. Firstly log in
    with your HZ account, then select the item or event you want,
    and complete the payment process digitally.

  4. How can you book a project space?

    Project spaces can be booked through the HZ booking system via the HZ website.
    Log in with your HZ account, pick an available project room, and reserve it for a set time.

  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    When you park your vehicle between the judicial building and the library,
    you can get a ticket from a nearby machine (usually located near the barier)
