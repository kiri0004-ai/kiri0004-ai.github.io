SWOT Analysis

Evaluating Personal and Academic Journey

This blog post discusses the SWOT analysis of my personal and academic journey. A SWOT analysis helps identify internal strengths and weaknesses, as well as external opportunities and threats.

STRENGTHS

  • critical thinking
  • Realism and Groundedness
  • Open-Minded Curiosity
  • Self-Awareness

WEAKNESSES

  • Excessive self-criticism
  • Crippling fear of Failure
  • Perfectionism That paralyzes Progress
  • Lack of direction and motivational Structure

OPPORTUNITIES

  • Informational interviews
  • Reframing environment
  • Gamify your self-study
  • Join a study group

THREATS

  • Potential failure
  • Sizure
  • Stress
  • Death
